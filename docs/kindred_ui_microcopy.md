# Kindred UI Microcopy Guide

## Purpose

This document translates the Kindred language system into product text.

Every word in the UI must:

- Feel quiet
- Feel obvious (in hindsight)
- Never instruct
- Never create pressure

---

## Global Rules

### 1. No urgency

Avoid:
- Don’t forget
- It’s been X days
- Reminder

### 2. No task language

Avoid:
- Task
- Complete
- To-do
- Follow up

### 3. No system language

Avoid:
- Manage
- Track
- Organize

---

## Core Actions

These are fixed. Do not rename.

- **Kindred** — home
- **Kin** — person view
- **Your Kin** — the grid
- **Keep** — add someone
- **Show Up** — your profile

---

## Empty States

### Kindred (no people yet)

> No one here yet.

Alternative:

> It starts with one.

---

### Notes (empty)

> Nothing here yet.

Avoid:
- Add your first note

---

### Dates (empty)

> No dates yet.

Alternative:

> Nothing marked.

---

## Add / Keep Flow

### Add manually

Button:
> Keep someone

Avoid:
- Add contact

---

### From profile

Button:
> Keep

Avoid:
- Add
- Follow
- Connect
- Join

---

### Invite

> Invite to Show Up

Avoid:
- Join Kindred
- Sign up
- Create an account

---

## Subtle Guidance (allowed, but indirect)

Instead of:

> Add a birthday

Use:

> Birthdays show up here.

---

Instead of:

> Write a note

Use:

> Things you want to remember.

---

## Error / Edge States

Keep neutral. No blame.

> That didn’t work.

> Try again.

Avoid:
- You entered something wrong

---

## Microcopy Patterns

### Recognition

> You’ve thought about them.

### Presence

> Keep them close.

### Neutral State

> It’s here when you need it.

---

## What NOT to Do

Bad:

> Stay on top of your relationships

> Never forget important dates

> Manage your contacts

Good:

> Keep them close

> They show up when they matter

---

## Final Rule

If the UI text feels like it’s helping too much,

it’s already too loud.
